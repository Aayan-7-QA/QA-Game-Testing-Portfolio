# [Sound][Shooting] During Gameplay, Shooting Sound 
Does Not Appear After Firing Some Bullets

## Description
When a player is playing a game, taking a gun 
from inventory/backpack and shooting some bullets, 
the gun sound does not appear as zero sound 
but the gameplay animation continues.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Launch the game on the build 000.121.67
2. Start the gameplay/mission
3. Take the gun from your inventory or backpack
4. Fire the gun by pressing the firing button

## Expected Result
After clicking the firing button, the shooting 
sound should be audible

## Actual Result
After pressing the firing button, the shooting 
sound is not audible and the animation continues

## Details
- **Platform:** PC
- **Severity:** High
- **Reproducibility:** 5/5
- **Labels:** Sound, Shooting, Gameplay
