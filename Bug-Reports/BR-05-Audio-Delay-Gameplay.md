# [Audio][Gameplay] Game Audio is Delayed or 
Does Not Play During Gameplay

## Description
The sound does not start playing right away while 
the user is playing a game. Delayed gunshot sounds 
appear late and happen intermittently during gameplay.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Launch the game on the build 90808.12122
2. Start the gameplay/mission
3. Set device audio to 70 to 90 percent
4. Play for a few minutes and trigger 
   shooting and walking

## Expected Result
Audio should be clear, without delays, play 
immediately and be synchronized with player action

## Actual Result
Audio does not play immediately and is delayed

## Details
- **Platform:** PC
- **Severity:** Medium
- **Reproducibility:** 5/5
- **Labels:** Audio, Gameplay
