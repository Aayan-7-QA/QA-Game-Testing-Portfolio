# [Gameplay][Crash] Game Crashes When Player Opens Map Repeatedly

## Description
When a player opens and closes the map 10 to 15 times 
rapidly while playing, the game crashes without 
displaying an error message.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Launch the game on the build 1.3.1234.678979
2. Start the gameplay
3. Open the map and close it 10 to 15 times quickly

## Expected Result
The map should open and close instantly 
without causing any crash

## Actual Result
The game crashes after about 10 to 15 times 
of opening and closing the map

## Details
- **Platform:** PC
- **Severity:** Critical
- **Reproducibility:** 5/5
- **Labels:** Gameplay, Crash
