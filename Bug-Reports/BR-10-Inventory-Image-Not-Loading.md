# [Inventory][Gameplay] When the Inventory Opens, 
the Item's Image Does Not Load

## Description
When playing the game, 
when the player opens the inventory, the image of the item fails to load,and 
empty boxes are visible.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Launch the game on the build 7890.91
2. Start the gameplay
3. Pick the game items such as guns and ammo
4. Open the inventory

## Expected Result
After opening the inventory, the images of 
the items should be visible

## Actual Result
After opening the inventory, images of the 
items fail to load and only empty boxes 
are displayed

## Details
- **Platform:** PC
- **Severity:** High
- **Reproducibility:** 5/5
- **Labels:** Inventory, Gameplay
